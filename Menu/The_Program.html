The Deal/Beginning

_____________________________________________ Once you reach lvl 15, you will be presented with a deal,
A Large Suitcase
2 week Rental in a Private Island
(This only available to New Recruits under lvl 15 with a networth under 5m)

This will start you on the path to making money...
You start with a capacity of 5 travel items, the Large Suitcase provides +4 to your item capacity, as well as +10 items for being in a Private Island, then a further +10 provided by the Faction's perks.
For a Total of 29 item capacity.....

Every few trips you make (less, if you need the money to fly with), you will trade your Plushies and Flowers to Phillip J Fry!

By selling to him, you get 98% market value, a safe, mug free trade with a receipt every time.

The Deal is, you pay back the roughly 30m for the Large Suitcase and the 2 weeks in the P.I.

As you fly and trade with PJFry, he will keep ¼ of your profits. Meaning if you make $1,000,000, he'll keep $250,000 and sends you $750,000.
You will join his Tabs server in order to keep track of your payments.
Everything is documented in your individual channel.

Simply put.....
Your Mission is to Fly & Trade to pay off your Loan.
